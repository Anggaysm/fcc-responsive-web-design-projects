The Beatles

British rock band

the following is a list of albums from The Beatles

  • Please Please Me the first album released by The Beatles in 1963, by Parlophone Records, following the success of the singles 'Love Me Do' (ranked 17) and 'Please Please Me' (ranked 1) in the UK charts.
  • With the Beatles second studio album by British band The Beatles. It was released on November 22, 1963, by Parlophone records, recorded four months after the release of the Beatles' debut album 'Please Please Me'.
  • A Hard Day’s Night The Beatles' third studio album, released on July 10, 1964, with one side containing songs from the soundtrack of their film A Hard Day's Night. The American version of the album was released two weeks earlier, on June 26, 1964 by United Artist Records, with a different track list.
  • Help! is the Beatles' fifth album in the UK and tenth in the United States. this album contains fourteen songs and seven of them are in their film. In 2012, the album was voted the 331st best album in Rolling Stone magazine.
  • Rubber Soul is the sixth studio album by British rock group The Beatles, released on 3 December 1965. Produced by George Martin, took just four weeks to make.
  • Revolver is The Beatles' seventh album, published on 5 August 1966. The album reached number 1 in the UK and US charts. This album is the true "magnum opus" of The Beatles. MOJO magazine called this album: “An epic, the highest achievement western music has ever achieved”
  • Sgt. Pepper’s Lonely Hearts Club Band an album released by The Beatles. It is The Beatles' 9th album released in the United Kingdom. It was released on June 1, 1967, and remained at the top of the charts for 26 weeks.
  • Abbey Road The 11th studio album released by the British rock band The Beatles. This was the last album they recorded, although Let It Be was the last album released before the band's disbandment in 1970.
  • Let It Be is the 12th and final studio album released by The Beatles. It was released on May 8, 1970 by Apple Records shortly after The Beatles announced their disbandment.
